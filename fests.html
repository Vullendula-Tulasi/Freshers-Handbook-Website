FESTS

SHRUTHI: THE CULTURAL FEST
The most anticipated event of the year, Shruthi, is the cultural fest of CBIT. Literati, held during first two days of Shruthi, is where you can participate in fun interactive events or even volunteer to organize. No matter where your interests lie, you are sure to relish three entertaining days filled with amazing food, musical nights and a beautiful campus.

SUDHEE: THE TECHNICAL FEST
The technical fest of CBIT, gives you a singular opportunity to showcase your proficiency in varied technical skills. Inaugurated by vibrant flashmobs, Sudhee offers you a wide range of events brought out by students of all the branches, replete with great incentives that are bound to tap the engineer in you.
